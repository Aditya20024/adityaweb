About the Event

Formula 1 (F1) racing events, or Grand Prix, are typically held over a weekend, starting with practice sessions on Friday and Saturday, followed by a qualifying session on Saturday to determine the starting order for the race on Sunday. The Grand Prix itself is the main event, with drivers battling it out to score points and win the race. ...
