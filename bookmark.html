About

The Kawasaki Ninja is a brand of bikes made by Kawasaki two wheeler manufacturer in India. There are 9 Ninja models on offer with price starting from Rs. 3,43,000 (avg. Ex-showroom). The cheapest model under the series is Kawasaki Ninja 300 with 296cc engine generating 38.88 bhp of power. Whereas the most expensive model is Kawasaki Ninja H2R with 998cc engine generating 305.75 bhp of power. Kawasaki Ninja 300 is a sports bike available in only 1 variant and 3 colours.
